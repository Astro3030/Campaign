Welcome to Echoes of Eldoria

A realm once thriving under ancient magic, now teeters on the brink of collapse. The balance of power is shifting, and your party holds the key to its fate.

Introduction

Echoes of Eldoria is a high-fantasy campaign where players embark on an epic journey across a world torn between powerful factions, lost magics, and the return of an ancient evil.

This campaign includes deep lore, dynamic NPC interactions, and unique world mechanics to enhance storytelling.

Major Characters

  • King Alden Valorcrest: The ruler of Valeria, desperate to maintain order as the world crumbles.
  • High Mage Seraphina: A powerful wizard seeking to recover the lost magic of the ancients.
  • Lord Veymar Blackthorn: A warlord and leader of the Iron Legion, determined to conquer Eldoria.
  • The Whisper: An enigmatic rogue who operates in the shadows, controlling information.
  • The Awakened One: A mysterious entity believed to be tied to the return of an ancient evil.

The World of Eldoria

Valeria: The capital city, seat of power and intrigue.

The Shattered Isles: Ruins of an ancient civilization, home to forgotten magic.

The Ashen Wastes: A cursed wasteland where nightmares walk.

Factions

  • The Radiant Order: A noble faction seeking to restore balance through law and magic.
  • The Shadowborn: A secretive cult obsessed with unlocking forbidden knowledge.
  • The Iron Legion: A militaristic force bent on conquest and order.

Campaign Mechanics

  • Faction Reputation System: Player actions influence relationships with major factions.
  • Ancient Magic: Unlock hidden spells and artifacts lost to time.
  • Exploration Challenges: Dynamic encounters based on the environment.
